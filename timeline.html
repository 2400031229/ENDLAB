Historical Timeline of India

Journey through India's rich historical heritage

3300-1300 BCE

Indus Valley Civilization

One of the world's earliest urban civilizations, known for advanced city planning, drainage systems, and trade networks.

322-185 BCE

Maurya Empire

Founded by Chandragupta Maurya, reached its peak under Ashoka the Great, who promoted Buddhism and non-violence.

320-550 CE

Gupta Age - Golden Period

Known as the Golden Age of India, marked by achievements in science, mathematics, astronomy, literature, and arts.

1206-1526 CE

Delhi Sultanate

Series of Muslim dynasties that ruled from Delhi, introducing Islamic culture and architecture to India.

1526-1857 CE

Mughal Empire

Founded by Babur, reached its zenith under Akbar, Shah Jahan, and Aurangzeb. Built iconic monuments like the Taj Mahal.

1858-1947

British Colonial Rule

British East India Company and later British Crown ruled India. Period of exploitation but also modernization and independence movements.

1947

Independence

India gained independence on August 15, 1947, led by Mahatma Gandhi's non-violent resistance movement.

1947-1991

Modern India Formation

Post-independence nation building, Green Revolution, space program, and establishment of democratic institutions.

1991-Present

Economic Liberalization & Digital India

Economic reforms, IT revolution, emergence as a global technology hub, and digital transformation initiatives.
